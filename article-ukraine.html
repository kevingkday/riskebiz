Political Risk Insurance for Canadian Companies Operating in Ukraine

As reconstruction partnerships and agricultural exports draw Canadian investment back into Ukraine, financial officers face a complex, high-friction risk environment where traditional property packages are invalid.

Canadian businesses participating in agricultural, energy, or infrastructure projects in Ukraine are encountering unprecedented risk profiles. Standard corporate policies, including global Commercial Property (CP) and Commercial General Liability (CGL) coverage, contain absolute war, invasion, and hostility exclusion clauses.

To protect overseas equipment, agricultural storage centers, and contract values, companies must turn to specialized international **Political Risk Insurance (PRI)** markets to secure the required capacity.

Three Core PRI Pillars to Secure

A viable Ukraine-specific PRI policy must be structured around three independent exposure headers:

  • Confiscation, Expropriation, and Nationalization (CEN): Protects physical machinery, storage tanks, and offices from direct government seizures or regulatory interference that permanently deprives the company of its assets.
  • Political Violence (PV): Replaces standard property damage policies. PV coverage triggers for physical asset destruction resulting from military activities, drone attacks, strikes, or sabotage.
  • Contract Frustration: Protects cash flows and trade finance setups if a government entity breaches a trade agreement or refuses to honor a structured sovereign payment.
"For Canadian exporters, political risk is no longer a secondary strategic consideration; it is the fundamental parameter that determines balance sheet stability in Central Europe."

The Underwriting Challenge

Priced on a case-by-case basis, PRI underwriters evaluate the specific physical proximity to active lines, security measures, and the presence of government-backed guarantees. In many cases, co-insurance arrangements involving the Export Development Canada (EDC) and private insurers are utilized to secure the necessary limits.
