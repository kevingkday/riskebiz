Demystifying Specialty Placements

Exporting and expanding overseas introduces a complex matrix of exposures. Standard brokers operate on volume, fitting companies into domestic packages that feature severe international exclusions. Host government interventions, currency blockages, foreign customer defaults, and overseas travel threats require a dedicated trade risk strategy.

Riskebiz was created as an educational resource to demystify the three critical, trade-related coverages that generalist brokers rarely discuss: Political Risk, Kidnap & Ransom, and Trade Credit. Our briefs help exporters identify policy gaps and approach the specialty market with confidence.

"Commercial operations can no longer afford to treat policy exclusions as administrative boilerplate. Exclusions are active liabilities that require strategic placement structures."
