Specialty vs. Standard Markets

Specialized Coverages

Standard commercial insurance packages are designed for predictable, domestic operational exposures. However, international supply chains, cyber extortion, political transitions, and high-liability projects introduce complexities that standard insurers explicitly exclude.

Riskebiz provides direct educational resources to structure coverage architectures utilizing global specialty syndicates—predominantly situated in London, Bermuda, and Singapore.
