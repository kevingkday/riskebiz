Specialty vs. Standard Markets

Export & Trade Coverages

Standard commercial insurance is designed for domestic, predictable business. But when you export or operate overseas, standard policies fail. Political interventions, foreign payment defaults, and international personnel security threats introduce risks that standard brokers rarely discuss and general commercial policies explicitly exclude.

Riskebiz provides direct educational resources to structure coverage architectures utilizing global specialty syndicates to secure your overseas trade.

public

Political Risk

Protects exporters, foreign investors, and international traders holding overseas assets, inventories, or equipment against host government interventions, expropriation, currency blockages, and contract frustration.

Key Exposures Covered
  • Asset Expropriation & Confiscation
  • Currency Inconvertibility
  • Forced Abandonment / War Damages

security

Kidnap & Ransom (K&R)

Secures international sales teams, supply chain personnel, and overseas executives against foreign extortion, kidnapping, and security threats with immediate professional crisis response.

Key Exposures Covered
  • Crisis Consulting Fees
  • Ransom & Extortion Funding
  • Emergency Evacuation & Medical

payments

Trade Credit

Protects export accounts receivable portfolios against overseas buyer insolvencies, protracted defaults, and political non-payment or currency transfer restrictions.

Key Exposures Covered
  • Buyer Insolvency
  • Protracted Commercial Default
  • Sovereign Debt Non-Payment
